Our group presented three papers at EACL 2026 in Rabat, Morocco: one at the Main conference, and two in Findings.

Main:
* [Form and Meaning in Intrinsic Multilingual Evaluations](https://aclanthology.org/2026.eacl-long.113/) by Wessel Poelman and Miryam de Lhoneux.

Findings:
* [ReBPE: Iteratively Improving the Internal Structure of a Structured Tokeniser by Mining its Internal Structure](https://aclanthology.org/2026.findings-eacl.211/) by Thomas Bauwens and Miryam de Lhoneux.
* [TIPA: Typologically Informed Parameter Aggregation](https://aclanthology.org/2026.findings-eacl.119/) by Stef Accou and Wessel Poelman.
