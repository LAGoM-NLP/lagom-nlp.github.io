Our group will present two papers at EMNLP, one oral presentations at the main conference, and one at the Multilingual Representation Learning Workshop.

* [Confounding Factors in Relating Model Performance to Morphology](https://aclanthology.org/2025.emnlp-main.369/) by Wessel Poelman\*, Thomas Bauwens\* and Miryam de Lhoneux.
* [Type and Complexity Signals in Multilingual Question Representations](https://aclanthology.org/2025.mrl-main.28/) by Robin Kokot and Wessel Poelman.
